About

Ever since I was young, video games have been a huge part of my life. Whether it was sharing laughs with friends, breaking games in unimaginable ways, or passing the controller back and forth between my older brother and me when our parents walked by because I was too young to play, games have always brought a special magic and joy that nothing else can match. Games mean so much — not just to me, but to millions of people in countless ways. I believe it is my mission in life to bring joy to people through this medium. Every challenge brings another opportunity to learn, and that feeling is almost as rewarding as finishing a project and bringing something new into the world for players to experience.
