Klein

Game Engineer and 3D Artist

About

Klein is a blobby beat-em-up party game inspired by games like Gang Beasts and Human Fall Flat. Two teams duke it out across different locations competing in various gamemodes as the world changes around them. Klein was made in part to study how the theories of group identity, deviance regulation, and descriptive norms stop or alter toxic behaviors while playing games.
